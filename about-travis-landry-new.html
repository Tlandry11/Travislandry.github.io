Why do I think learning frontend development skills is valuable for a designer?

It is important to know about HTML, CSS, and Javascript to understand how design elements function. Also, it improves the communication between designers and developers using program language. This gives designers the understanding what deliverables the developers need to avoid confusion and other issues when collaborating.
